About Me

Hi, I am a multimedia artist - my work ranges from illustration, painting and mural art; to design and media. Currently I am in my final year reading for a degree in Digital Arts at the University of Malta. The words ‘create’ and ‘design’ have always sparked a sense of enthusiasm and excitement within me and the course gave me the opportunity to expand my portfolio greatly. Through these past three years I have enjoyed designing logos, packaging and coming up with creative concepts.
